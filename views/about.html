Cards by Cloud

This idea blossomed from a very respectable employee who had a hard time getting business cards through his employer. The managers would constantly use cost as a justification why they would not let us order business cards. That experience proved that it was not possible to order or for that matter use business cards when they were needed.

This created awkward situations when you meet new people and they would ask for a business card. This is where the idea for Cards by Cloud™ was born. We may forget business cards, but we always have our smart phones with us. Why not have a App that allows us to exchange business cards whereby not only can one send his or her virtual business card to someone else, but someone else can request your business card. This relieves the person that wants to give his or her business card out to several people from having to individually send the virtual business card.

We are interested in eliminating or drastically reducing the number of business cards that are being used by businesses. It makes no sense today, more than 500 years after Gutenberg invented the printing press, that we should use this ancient technology to convey our contact information to each other.
